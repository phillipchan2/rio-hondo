The Rio Hondo VITA program has helped more than 340 people in our local community since 2011!

The Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) program is a certificate program offered in conjunction with the IRS and the California Franchise Tax Board.

Students that complete this program can volunteer to participate in the college’s VITA Days, assisting community customers in filing their state and federal income tax returns.

“We save the community about $50,000 or more in tax preparation fees...and it brings our students great pride in what they do.” - Jeannie Liu MBA, CMA, EA
